My greatest treasure My Family

I come from a loving, hardworking family. Being the oldest child taught me responsibility and accountability, principles that I have had to apply daily to succeed. I am definitely a family guy who loves to spend time with family, they are simply my greatest motivation, specially my wife, Fabianna, who inspires me with her example to be a better man.

What I like the most Soccer

Ever since I was little, my dad would take me to watch live soccer games. That changed me and until this day I enjoy everything about soccer. I love talking about my favorite team, watching them play against any team, playing soccer, and teaching others principles and game strategies.

My best investment Traveling

It is fascinating to me to learn about different cultures, gastronomies, beliefs, traditions, and places. There is something magical about traveling, which is why I enjoy recording videos to document every moment.
